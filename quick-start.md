## 📥 Adding Razor

To begin your journey, you must invite **Razor** to your Server. 

[ **Click Here to Add Razor** ](https://discord.com/api/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1143862168253644860&permissions=303600576574&scope=bot%20applications.commands)

Once added, head to any channel and try running `/bot ping` to verify the connection!

---

## 🔑 Required Permissions

<Warning>
  **Mandatory Administrator Access**
  
  To function correctly, the **Administrator** permission is highly recommended. If you choose to manually assign permissions, the bot **will fail** without the core capabilities listed below.
</Warning>

If you prefer custom configurations, ensure Razor has the following minimum permissions:

1. **Manage Guild** — Required to configure server-based settings.
2. **Manage Server** — Required for automated systems.
3. **Manage Roles** — Crucial for Autorole, Economy Shop, and Reaction Roles.
4. **Ban/Kick Members** — Essential for the Automod Plus system.
5. **View Audit Log** — Necessary for the advanced logging system.
