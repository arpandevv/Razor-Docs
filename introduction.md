### Further Capabilities:
- **📈 Member Analytics**: Real-time server growth tracking with visual data plots on your dashboard.
- **🎭 Reaction Roles**: Effortless button and dropdown menus for dynamic role assignment.
- **🎁 Power Giveaways**: Advanced giveaway hosting with required roles and voice-channel configurations.
- **📝 Smart Logging**: A comprehensive server logging system equipped with a searchable history database.
- **🎵 Crystal Music**: High-fidelity music playback with extensive playlist support.
- **🤖 Imagine / AI**: Dynamic image generation and sophisticated AI interactions.
- **👋 Welcomify**: Beautiful, deeply customizable join and leave greetings via the web interface.
