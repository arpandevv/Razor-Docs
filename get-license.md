To use the full capabilities of **Razor**, you need a valid license key. Follow this step-by-step guide to retrieve, activate, and manage your license.

---

## 🔑 Acquiring a License Key

Follow these precise steps to sync your purchases and request a license key from the development team.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the Official Support Server">
    First, ensure you are a member of our Discord community:
    
    <Card title="Join Support Server" icon="discord" color="#5865F2" href="https://discord.gg/YSv9VqQg6g">
      Click here to join the official Razor Support Server.
    </Card>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sync Your Account">
    1. Navigate to the [#🛠️︱bot-commands](https://discord.com/channels/1274788237780455496/1274788238460194900) channel in our Discord server.
    2. Execute the slash command to synchronize your purchase role:
       ```text
       /sync
       ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open a License Ticket">
    1. Go to the [#🎫︱ticket-support](https://discord.com/channels/1274788237780455496/1280229197129978049/1374435684223291573) channel.
    2. Click on the dropdown menu under **Select a ticket type** and select **Get License Key**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for Key Issuance">
    Once your ticket is created, wait for a project manager or staff member to claim the ticket. They will verify your purchase status and issue your unique license key directly in the ticket.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## 🌐 Managing Your Licenses

You can view, manage, and transfer all of your issued license keys through our secure online portal.

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Razor License Portal" icon="globe" href="https://license.razorbot.online/">
    Visit **[license.razorbot.online](https://license.razorbot.online/)** to view your active license keys, check expiration dates, and manage associated servers.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Ensure you keep your license key secure and never share it publicly or with unauthorized administrators.
</Note>
